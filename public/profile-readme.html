README: HackTech Operator Profile

This profile README mirrors the intent of the original HackTech introduction: demonstrate a disciplined, ethics-first approach to cyber intelligence while showcasing the breadth of educational resources curated for the community.

Mission Statement

HackTech exists to elevate defenders, researchers, and policy shapers with timely intelligence. We dissect breaches, catalogue tooling, and provide safe environments for experimentation—never encouraging unauthorized access.

What You'll Find

  • Daily intelligence briefs backed by Workers AI fallbacks for continuity.
  • Breach archives with lessons learned for IR playbooks and tabletop drills.
  • Tooling roundups that emphasize lawful usage, detection, and hardening.
  • Contact workflows using Cloudflare Pages Functions and MailChannels.

Operating Guidelines

  • Respect legal boundaries—every capability showcased is for sanctioned research.
  • Prioritize transparency: document methodologies and share defensive indicators.
  • Champion accessibility so new analysts can on-ramp quickly and ethically.
  • Secure communications: prefer encrypted channels and vetted infrastructure.

Connect & Contribute

Ready to collaborate? Use the contact form to propose research partnerships, submit detection analytics, or surface threat reports that deserve amplification. Responsible disclosure is mandatory; HackTech will never publish exploit code without accompanying mitigations.
