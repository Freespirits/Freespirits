HackTech now ships with the Lobe Midjourney Web UI so operators can craft visual intelligence directly from the command deck. Configure your Midjourney proxy once and orchestrate prompts, upscale jobs, and review image generations without leaving the grid.

Deploy & Connect
  1. Launch the midjourney-proxy service (Docker makes it a one-command deploy) and populate application.yml with your Discord guild, channel, and token credentials.
  2. Expose the proxy endpoint (for example, https://your-proxy.example.com) and confirm /mj responds with the available APIs.
  3. Open the settings panel inside the Midjourney deck, paste the proxy URL, and authenticate. The UI immediately syncs queued jobs and prompt presets.
