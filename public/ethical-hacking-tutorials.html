Defensive Countermeasures

Blue Team
  • Complete the SANS Blue Team Operations exercises or the open Sigma rules workshops to sharpen detection engineering.
  • Deploy the Elastic Security or Wazuh stack in a lab to practice log ingestion, correlation, and automated response.

Operational Playbooks

Process
